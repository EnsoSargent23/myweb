Eyüp Süyrüge

I am a student at Hochschule Rhein-Waal, currently in my 3rd year studying Mediainformatics. I was born and raised in Moers, but I have Turkish roots from both sides of my family. My educational journey has been a continuous upward curve, starting with a regular elementary school, followed by a secondary school and high school. After graduating from high school, I went to France to study at a private university. Interestingly, even though I attended a French high school, I never learned the French language. Instead, I learned the Arabic language, which might seem ironic. After a long search, I've found a university and a course that I'm really excited about. It connects with things I loved as a kid, and I can't wait to dive into this new chapter of learning. I'm looking forward to putting my heart into something that truly matters to me. I enjoy learning new things, communicating with others and developing new ideas. My religious beliefs are an important part of my life. Respecting each person's boundaries is important to me because it helps prevent unintentional harm.

Bal Mumu

Bal Mumu Denizlerim, dolar ve taşar,
en aydınlık gecelerimde bile, Sabah’ a kadar
Fitilim yok olali cok zaman oldu yar
Kalp Damarlarımdan Bal Mumu akar
Yakamoz ısıkları sarar akıl denizimi
En derinlerden gün yüzune cıkar
Sabir Taşlarından yaptım Kalp Evlerimi
Ikiye ayrıldıkca Bal Mumu Şelalelerim coşar.
Mum Isiği ile çokca geceledik, Hasret Nöbetlerinde
Tek Kanatlı diye Lakablandırılmısız zaar
Tabibler ümidi kesince ikimizdende
Şifa ile Zehri ayni Şişede sundular yar.

Kerzenlichter

Ich sah Kerzenlichter am Horizont,
flackerten hin und her,
in Harmonie zu tiefem Meer,
übertrugen ihre letzte Kraft,
an die Sprösslinge ihrer Gefolgschaft.

Ich sah Kerzenlichter an Fensterbänke stehen,
sehnsuchtsvoll nach verlorene Schätze sehen,
Erinnerungsstücke verteilt hier und da,
nichts blieb so wie es einmal war.

Ich sah Kerzenlichter ausgebrannt,
ausgesetzt dem Windesheer,
im Angesicht der starken Böen,
sich ergebend zum Gewähr.

Ich sah Kerzenlichter aufrechtstehen,
und sogar die kleinste Hoffnung sehen,
mit Hingabe und purer Leidenschaft,
welches die Flamme der Herzen entfacht.

Ich sah Kerzenlichter summen,
ein Lied der Trostlosigkeit,
die Augen vor Tränen verschwommen,
wegen der sinnlosen Streitigkeit.

Ich sah Kerzenlichter im Angesicht des Regens,
von den Regenrinnen gleitend hin zum Saat
für die unausweichliche Wahrheit des Lebens,
bestreitend nach langer Reise den letzten Pfad.
